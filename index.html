Universal Computer Project

One device to power all of your experiences. Wired, wireless, and over the internet.

The Universal Computer project defines a set of protocols and standards by which a computer can interact with various hardware.

The goal is to consolidate compute for the following types of devices:

  • Phone
  • Tablet
  • Laptop
  • PC
  • Smart watch
  • Smart TV
  • VR/AR headset
  • Smart speaker
  • Smart appliances
  • Home automation
  • Vehicle infotainment
